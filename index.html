Calypso Rose

The Mother of Calpyso

Lets take a look at the life and achievement of the Mother of Calpyso

  • 1940 - Born in Bethel Village, Tobago, Trinidad and Tobago
  • 1950's - Calpyso Rose began singing at Calpyso Tents at the age of 15
  • 1963 - Won the Calypso King contest in St.Thomas with her first recording
  • 1964 - She decided to dedicate her life to a career in music. Although originally known as Crusoe Kid, she was given the name Calypso Rose by calypsonian Mighty Spoiler and fellow Carnival tent members.
  • 1966 - Rose wrote the song "Fire in Me Wire", the first calypso ever running two years in a row at the Trinidad Carnival
  • 1967 - She performed with Bob Marley & the Wailers at the Grand Ballroom in New York City
  • 1978 - The Trinidad Road March competition's title was changed to Calypso Monarch in her honor.
  • 1983 - Rose has resided in New York since 1983.
  • 1996 - 1998 - During this period, Rose underwent surgery for breast cancer & treatment for a malignancy in her stomach
  • 2015 - French singer Manu Chao discovered her work and together with Ivan Duran produced her album Far From Home.
  • 2016 - A compilation, Calypso Rose, Queen of Calypso for 40 years!, was released in 2016.
  • 2017 - The album won the 2017 World Music Album of the Year prize at French music award ceremony Les Victoires de la Musique and was awarded Platinum sale status in France, a premiere for any artist from Trinidad and Tobago
  • 2019 - Rose performed at Coachella, marking the first time a calypso performer played a full set at the festival. At 78, she became the festival's oldest performer to date
